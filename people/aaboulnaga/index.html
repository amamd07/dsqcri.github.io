Short bio / Detailed CV

Research

I am a Research Director in the Qatar Computing Research Institute (QCRI), which is part of the Hamad Bin Khalifa University. I head the Distributed Systems Group at QCRI. I am also an Adjunct Associate Professor at the University of Waterloo. My current research focuses on the following areas:
  • Graph processing and mining
  • Distributed RDF and linked data
  • Elastic scale out of database workloads
  • Storage infrastructure for database systems
